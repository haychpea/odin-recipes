Chocolate Fudge Recipe

Description

Chocolate fudge is a smooth, creamy confection made with sugar, butter, milk, and chocolate. It has a rich chocolate flavor and is often enjoyed on its own or as a topping for desserts such as ice cream. It is a popular treat among both children and adults.

This guide will show you how to make this decadent treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups White Sugar
  • 7 oz Marshmallow Creme
  • 2/3 cup Evaporated Milk
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 2 cups Milk Choc Chips
  • 1 cup Dark Choc Chips
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Nuts
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Method

  1. Line an 8-inch square pan with aluminum foil; set aside.
  2. Combine sugar, marshmallow cream, evaporated milk, butter, and salt together in a large saucepan over medium heat; bring to a full boil and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  3. Remove from heat and add milk chocolate chips and dark chocolate chips; stir until chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth. Stir in nuts and vanilla. Pour into prepared pan; chill in refrigerator for 2 hours, or until firm.
